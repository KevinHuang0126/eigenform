EigenForm Privacy Policy

Effective July 17, 2026

EigenForm is an iOS app that uses your device's camera to analyze exercise form in real time. This policy describes what information the app handles and what happens to it.

Camera video stays on your device

EigenForm uses the camera solely to detect your body position during a workout. All video is processed on your device using Apple's on-device Vision framework. Video is never recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere, and no one — including us — can view it. Spoken coaching cues are generated with on-device speech synthesis; the microphone is never used.

Information we collect

Account information. When you create an account we collect your email address, an optional display name, and, if you sign in with Apple or Google, the identifier those services provide. This is used only to sign you in and identify your data.

Workout history. After each session the app saves a summary to your account: the exercise performed, repetition count, duration, any form-fault notes, and the date and time. No video or images are included.

Local data. If a workout summary cannot be uploaded immediately, it is kept on your device and retried later.

How your information is used

Your information is used only to operate the app: signing you in and showing you your own workout history. We do not use your data for advertising or marketing, we do not sell or share it with third parties, and the app contains no ads, analytics, or tracking.

Where your data is stored

Account and workout data are stored with Supabase, our database and authentication provider, which processes data on our behalf. Access is restricted so that each account can only read and write its own records. Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS.

Data retention and deletion

Your data is kept until you delete it. You can delete your account and all associated workout history at any time from within the app (Account, then Delete Account). Deletion is immediate and permanent. You can also request deletion by email at the address below.

Children

EigenForm is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date.
