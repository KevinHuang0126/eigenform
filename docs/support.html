Getting the best tracking

EigenForm needs a clear view of your body to track your form. Prop your phone in portrait a few feet away and make sure you're well lit.

  • Squat and pushup: stand side-on to the camera. The app will tell you if you're facing the wrong way.
  • Pullup: face the camera with your head and both hands visible.
  • Bicep curl: face the camera with your whole arm in frame.

Frequently asked questions

Reps aren't being counted. Make sure your whole body (or whole arm, for curls) stays in frame for the full movement, and check that the skeleton overlay is tracking you steadily. Poor lighting, baggy clothing, or standing too close to the camera are the most common causes. Only one person should be in frame.

The camera preview is black. EigenForm needs camera permission to work. Go to Settings, then EigenForm, and turn on Camera.

I don't hear the spoken cues. Check that your ringer switch isn't silencing media and that the volume is up. Cues duck your music rather than pausing it, so they play alongside whatever you're listening to.

Is my video recorded or uploaded? No. All analysis happens on your device and video is never recorded, stored, or transmitted. See the privacy policy for details.

How do I delete my account? In the app, go to Account, then Delete Account. Deletion is immediate and permanent, and removes your workout history. You can also request deletion by email.

Requirements
